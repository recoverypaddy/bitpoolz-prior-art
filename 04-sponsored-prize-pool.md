# DISCLOSURE 4: Sponsored Prize Pool Mechanism

## Technical Field
Third-party sponsored prize pools in Bitcoin Lightning sweepstakes systems.

## Background
Brands and sponsors may wish to fund prize pools in exchange for participant exposure, creating a new revenue model for the platform and free/subsidised entry for participants.

## Detailed Description
A prize pool variant wherein a verified third-party sponsor deposits sats (via Lightning invoice or on-chain) that fund all or part of the prize pool target. Participants enter for free (email only) or at reduced cost. The sponsor receives: branding display (logo, name, tagline) on pool card and all related UI, proportional exposure metric (impressions per sat sponsored, tracked and reported), post-draw winner announcement co-branding. The system handles: sponsor verification and KYC, sponsorship amount locking (sponsor sats held in reserve until draw or refunded if pool fails to reach minimum), participant free-entry allocation (fixed number of free entries per sponsored pool, determined by sponsorship amount), sponsor-to-prize conversion rate (90% of sponsor deposit to prize, 10% platform fee), public audit trail of sponsor contributions in the ledger, and tax documentation for sponsor (charitable sponsorship receipt if applicable).

## Key Claims
- Third-party sponsor funding mechanism for prize pools
- Sponsor verification and deposit locking
- Free participant entry allocation from sponsorship
- Sponsor branding display proportional to contribution
- Impressions-per-sat exposure metric
- Sponsor contribution in public ledger
- 90/10 sponsor-to-prize/platform split
